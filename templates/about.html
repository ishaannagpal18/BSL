BSL Mission

BSL- Brands and Sourcing Leaders Association, is a non-profit organization that represents Sourcing Leaders from the Domestic and Exports. We as a body have a comprehensive vision to bridge the gap between government policies and the apparel industry by guiding the policy makers about the advanced needs of the industry.
The association is formed to accelerate the growth of the Apparel Industry in the Country, and make the whole world wear “Make in India”.

Journey Till Now

Brands and Sourcing Leaders Association

BSL represents over USD 30 Billion of Domestic and International Buying of Textiles & Apparel. BSL is the only association that brings together international & Domestic brand Leaders.

Our members include Brands and Sourcing Leaders representing 250+Brands and Sourcing Houses like Marks & Spencer, ZARA, H&M, Aditya Birla, Arvind fashion, Triburg, Newtimes, PDS Group, Spykar, Nike, Adidas, French Connection UK, Li&fung, Next, Bestsellers, Myntra, GAP, Macy’s, Target, Jockey, Landmark, Nykaa, to name a few.

We have successfully conducted many conferences in Delhi and Bangalore. Glocal Textile Summit held in Delhi on 16th September 2022, had a large Industry leaders’ presence & representation from delegates from the Ministry of Textiles making it a successful and effective event.

BSL has also opened International chapters globally - USA(East coast), USA(West coast), Japan, UK, UAE, France, more are under process.
