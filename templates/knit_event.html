BSL is aggressively promoting MMF Textiles in Tirupur

The MMF garment industry in India is poised for fast growth as the government has agreed to promote the sector, according to Padmashri Dr. A Sakthivel, who spoke at the IKF Press Meet's opening.

The government has announced a Production Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme for the MMF segment and the establishment of seven mega textile parks in the nation. These actions will boost India's MMF garment exports and manufacture.

"MMF has a $200 billion global potential. Sanjay Shukla from Triburg and active Board Member of BSL stated, "We can target 10% of the $200 billion if we work together as a nation to build a supply chain that begins with design, product development, fabric development, and of course, garmenting.
